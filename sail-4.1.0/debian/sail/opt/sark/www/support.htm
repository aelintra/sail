Getting support

  • Community Support

    You can get free support from the on-line community wiki pages at sailpbx.com where you will find useful information on installing, managing and maintaining your sail PBX system

  • Professional Support

    If you already have a support contract you can call or email the support centre to seek advice or open a ticket. You will be required to quote the serial number shown on the globals page of your system or, for Sark200 or Sark500, the MAC address of your appliance

    If you do not have a support contract you can subscribe for one or you can purchase support hours on a pay-as-you-go basis. You will need a major credit card or a PayPal account. Click the button below to purchase support hours. Once you have received your confirmation email, you can contact the support centre quoting your order reference number and we will open a ticket for you.
