SAIL PBX

Getting support

  • Community Support

    You can get free support from the on-line community wiki pages at sailpbx.com where you will find useful information on installing, managing and maintaining your sail PBX system

  • Professional Support

    If you already have a support contract you can call or email the support centre to seek advice or open a ticket.
    You will be required to quote the serial number shown on the globals page of your system or, for Sark200/Sark500,
    the MAC address of your appliance

    If you do not have a support contract you can subscribe, or you can purchase support hours on a pay-as-you-go (PAYG) basis by contacting us.
    You will need a major credit card or a PayPal account for PAYG support.
